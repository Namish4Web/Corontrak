Let's get started with Corontrak.

Your help is our happiness. Let's help you track Covid cases in Corontrak. In Corontrak's website that is corontrak.web.app, you should see three tabs, home and covid cases and records and meet now. If you want to get introduction of Corontrak, just stay where you are and in case you want to track Covid-19, you have to click on Covid cases and records. If you are unable to do it, send us a email at corontrak.query@gmail.com and we will help you out. Now when you have clicked that, something with a horizontal object moving slowly and slowly, this is the progress bar which means that it is fetching data from our server.
